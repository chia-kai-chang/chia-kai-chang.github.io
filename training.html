Welcome to the Lab

Welcome to our research team! This manual will help you quickly understand how the lab operates, its guidelines, and available resources to get you started on your research journey.

Lab Guidelines

  • Attend the weekly Lab Meeting
  • Report research progress regularly
  • Return equipment after use and log usage
  • Keep the lab clean and organized
  • Respect intellectual property rights and cite sources properly

Common Tools

  • Python / Jupyter Notebook
  • Git / GitHub
  • Overleaf / LaTeX

Key Resources

  • Paper Databases (IEEE, ACM, Google Scholar)
  • NCU Library Electronic Resources

Onboarding Checklist

  1. Meet with the advisor to discuss research directions
  2. Join the lab communication group
  3. Obtain lab access permissions
  4. Familiarize yourself with lab equipment and usage policies
  5. Set up your development environment (Python, Git, etc.)
  6. Read the assigned foundational literature
  7. Attend your first Lab Meeting
